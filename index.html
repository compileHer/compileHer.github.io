Events

HACKATHON

DesignHer Hacks

Our first designHer hacks took place on Sunday, January 14th, 2018. It is an 8-hour event, which is intended to help middle school girls understand the impact they can make on the world by combining creative thinking with technology. Registration for our 2019 hackathon will be up in December. Read more about last year's hackathon here.

TECH CAPSTONE

Tech Capstone

Know any middle school girls in the Chicagoland area with an interest in computers and technology? Join us on in April 2019 (exact date TBA) for our fifth annual capstone event! Check back in March for information to register!

WORKSHOPS

Workshops

CompileHer holds coding workshops for local middle schoolers. If you're interested in getting your school involved, please email us at contact@compileher.com!

We seek to engage middle school girls in hands-on activities in the field of computer science in an effort to close the technology gender gap.
