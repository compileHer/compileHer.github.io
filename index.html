greenHer Hacks 2020: To a greenHer future

This past winter, compileHer held our annual hackathon! 74 sixth-to-eighth grade students from 29 different schools in the Chicagoland area came to the University of Chicago to attend our hackathon. Working in groups of three to five, students developed and pitched an idea for a mobile app that will create a "greenHer" future. From an interactive game that promotes recycling to an app that connects with IoT devices around the house to monitor each family member's carbon footprint, students expressed their creativity and learned to transform their ideas into something tangible.

Check out some of the photos of the event in our gallery! Special thanks to our keynote speaker Juanita Garcia, everyone who volunteered, all our attendees, and congratulations to the following award recipients:

  • Green and Clean: Group 12 (QuickSip)
  • Most Artsy: Group 8 (Laurel)
  • EmpowHer: Group 7 (Community, Clothing, Charity)
  • Wildest Imagination: Group 10 (Aquasavr)
  • TechSuperstars: Group 3 (Watts Down)
