Fall 2016 Coding Workshop



compileHer is a UChicago organization dedicated to engaging middle school girls in Computer Science in an effort to increase gender parity. We focus specifically on bringing hands-on CS activities to young girls in the Hyde Park community.

Last fall compileHer held an 8-week coding workshop to teach 7th and 8th grade girls Python programming skills Wednesdays after school at Kenwood Academy. The program will met weekly on Wednesdays from 4:20pm to 6:20pm beginning October 5th and ending November 30th (Nov 23rd was off in observance of Thanksgiving holidays).


Please email contact@compileher.com with any questions.
